Planetary gear: Input shaft attached to sun gear and ring gear, output shaft attached to carrier and planet gears.

Three cases: 1. Ring gear stationary, sun and carrier moving. Output speed positive. 2. Sun gear stationary, ring and carrier moving. Output speed 2. > 1. 3. Carrier stationary, sun and ring moving. Output speed negative.

Bases: object

A 2-degree of freedom shaft object A shaft with a constant circular cross-section can be defined using length and diameter values. Other types of shafts are defined using stiffness and moment of inertia values. Giving either the stiffness or inertia value overrides all stiffness and inertia calculations.
